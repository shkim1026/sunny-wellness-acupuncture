About

Dr. Sunny Sun Eui Kim

Sunny Sun Eui Kim, Ph.D./L. Ac., is an expert in Traditional Oriental Medicine. With over 20 years of experience, Dr. Kim has effectively treated patients with women's health issues such as infertility, miscarriages, menstrual disorders & cramps, cystic ovaries, uterine fibroids, PMS and menopause symptoms, as well as, weight Loss.

Dr. Kim is also a specialist in treating headache, shoulder pain, carpal tunnel syndrome, low back pain, knee pain and neck pain. She can help you restore, revitalize and maintain a healthy balance in your body in addition to improving your quality of life.

Dr. Kim graduated with honors from Emperor's College of Traditional Oriental Medicine in Santa Monica, California and has been practicing Oriental Medicine in the Los Angeles area since 1998.
